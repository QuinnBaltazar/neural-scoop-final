Neural Scoop Ice Cream Shop

At Neural Scoop, we’re a little bit obsessed with matching people to the perfect scoop.

We use rich flavors, real ingredients, and a tiny neural network that learns what you like. Whether you’re into classic vanilla, chocolate overload, or unhinged flavor experiments, our shop is built to help you find “that one” ice cream.
